About the map: this is a small map with arena theme, its perfect for 1vs1 or 2vs2...but i still thinks its 
funny to play with more people, it gets kinda messy.
I have used dmc textures (that wad file is great!) to get q2 feeling. 
If you have some time over have a look at the top sky, isnt the clouds moving?
