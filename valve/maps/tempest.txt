INSTALLATION:

Run Winzip and unzip tempest.zip.  Open the tempest folder and place the tempest.bsp, tempest.res, and tempest.txt in your valve/maps folder.  Place the 6 (six) titan256_.tga files and readme into your valve/gfx/env folder.  Place the  wav files in a folder called eclipse and place that in your valve/sounds folder.  Place the tempest overview files in your valve/overviews folder.     Open the overviews folder and place the tempest.bsp and tempest.txt in your valve/overviews folder.  Open the model folder and place the xeno.mdl and lavaburn.mdl in your valve/models/player folder.

If you'd like a copy of the metal.wad or vwad.wad, email me and I'll send it to you.  docrock64@cox.net
