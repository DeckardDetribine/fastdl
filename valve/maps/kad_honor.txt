INSTALLATION:

1. 	Run Winzip and unzip kad_honor.zip.

2. 	Open the kad_honor folder and place the kad_honor.bsp, 					kad_honor.res, and kad_honor.txt in your valve/maps folder.  

3.	Place the 6 (six) eunuch_.tga files into your valve/gfx/env folder.

4.	Place the wav file in a folder called kad and place that in your valve/sound 			folder.

5.	Place the flare55.spr, bi2.spr, and bluejet1.spr in your valve/sprites folder.
