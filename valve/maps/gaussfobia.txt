************
Introduction
************
    This map is made for who like playing with a Gauss. All the players respwan in an specific area of the map
 (an Lower level) and the gauss can be found in the higher levels. When a player is in an higher level, he
 has some advantages from who is on a lower.. so, you need to stay up and armored with the gauss to get
 a fast locomotion in this map.
