INSTALLATION:

Run Winzip and unzip greyscale.zip onto your desktop.  Open the greyscale folder and place the greyscale.bsp, greyscale.res, and greyscale.txt in your valve/maps folder.  Place the 6 (six) dfdmist.tga files into your valve/gfx/env folder.  Place the 3 wav files in a folder called grey and place that in your valve/sounds folder.  Place the greyscale overview files in your valve/overviews folder.  Place the BWsniper.mdl and BWsniper.bmp in a folder called BWsniper and place that folder in your valve/models/player folder.  Place the greyrat.mdl in your valve/models folder.

If you'd like a copy of the bw.wad, email me and I'll send it to you.  docrock64@cox.net
