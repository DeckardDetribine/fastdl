INSTALLATION:

Run Winzip and unzip sandspyre.zip.  Open the sandspyre folder and place the sandspyre.bsp, sandspyre.res, and sandspyre.txt in your valve/maps folder.   Place the files inside the gfx/env folder (6 desnoon.tga files) inside your Valve/gfx/env folder.  Take the stone sound folder and place it in your valve/sound folder.  And take the flame.spr and place it in the valve/sprites folder.  And place the sandspyre overview files in the valve/overview folder.

If you'd like a copy of the rocks.wad, email me and I'll send it to you.  docrock64@cox.net
