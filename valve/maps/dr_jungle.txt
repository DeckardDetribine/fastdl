INSTALLATION:

1. 	Run Winzip and unzip dr_jungle.zip.

2. 	Place the dr_jungle.bsp and dr_jungle.txt in your valve/maps folder.  

3.	You'll need to take the gfx folder and open it.  Then, inside there will be a folder called env.  		Place the files from the env folder [six swamp2.tga files] into your valve/gfx/env folder.

4.	Open the sound folder.  Place the folder called forest in your valve/sound folder.

5.	Open the sprite folder. Place the fixflame.spr in your valve/sprites folder.  If they are already 	there, do not replace them.
